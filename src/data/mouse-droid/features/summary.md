A short, always-current read on what you're working on right now — perfect for when you
come back from a meeting and need to reload your own context.

Mouse Droid rebuilds the summary from your most recent activity, refreshing it every few
minutes while you're at the keyboard. When you're away — locked, asleep, or simply idle —
he leaves it be, so it always reflects real work rather than a screensaver.

You'll see a compact version at the top of the **Timeline** tab and in the **tray menu**
(alongside your current project, desk time, and wellness score). "Read full summary"
opens the whole thing. It's written in the droid's voice; if he hasn't seen enough
activity yet, he'll simply report that he's standing by.

_Find it in the tray menu and at the top of the **Timeline** tab._
