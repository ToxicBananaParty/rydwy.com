Ask the droid about your day. Chat answers are grounded in what you've actually been
doing — not the model's imagination — and they stream in token by token as he thinks.

Before answering, Mouse Droid pulls together a digest of your recent activity and, when
project awareness is on, a brief dossier on your current project (its status, open work,
and blockers) straight from your vault. If a question needs more, he can reach into the
Memory Palace and search your notes by meaning, then answer from what he finds.

So you can ask things like *"what did I spend the morning on?"*, *"where did I leave the
deploy?"*, or *"what's blocking the billing project?"* and get a reply rooted in your real
timeline and notes. With Google Calendar connected and Meeting artifacts enabled, you can
also ask about your meetings' notes and action items — and, if the Transcript tab switch
is on, ask chat to read the transcript on demand.

**With CLI routing on** (Settings → AI & Models), chat keeps a real ongoing session: history and
context accumulate across your messages, the palace is available as a native tool, and the
conversation survives a restart. The **Clear** button starts a fresh session and erases the
stored transcript from disk.

**The input box is multi-line.** Press **Enter** to send, **Shift+Enter** to add a new line,
and **Esc** to clear the box. **Cmd+Enter** (or **Ctrl+Enter** on Linux/Windows) also sends.

**While the droid is working** you'll see a pulsing "…" in his reply bubble, and a
"Used N tools" count that ticks up live as he consults the palace or your connected
tools. Changed your mind? The **Send** button becomes **Stop** while he's replying —
tap it to cut him off; whatever he'd said so far stays in the thread.

**Switching tabs never loses your place.** If a reply is still streaming when you leave the
Chat tab, it keeps arriving in the background — come back and it's all there. Any draft you
were typing is held while you're away, and it survives an app restart too.

**Long conversations condense gracefully.** When a conversation grows past the model's
context window, Mouse Droid summarises the earliest messages and drops them in an inline
notice ("earlier messages summarised"). Tap it to expand and read what was folded in.

Chat speaks in the droid's voice. If you'd rather a plain, neutral tone for something you
plan to share, the **Reports** tab has a neutral-voice toggle.

_Find it in the **Chat** tab._
